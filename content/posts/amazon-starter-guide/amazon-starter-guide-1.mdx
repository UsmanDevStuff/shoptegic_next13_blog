## Prerequisites

Starting an Amazon business can be a great way to make money and build a successful online business. However, there are some important prerequisites that you need to consider before taking the plunge. Here are some of the key prerequisites for starting an Amazon business:

1. **Research**: Before you start your Amazon business, it’s important to do your research. You need to understand the market, the competition, and what products are selling well on Amazon. This will help you determine which products you should focus on and how to price them competitively.

2. **Business Plan**: A business plan is essential for any successful business venture, and this is especially true when it comes to starting an Amazon business. Your plan should include a detailed description of your product or service, pricing strategy, marketing plan, financial projections, and more.

3. **Capital**: Starting an Amazon business requires capital in order to purchase inventory and cover other expenses such as advertising costs and shipping fees. You may need to secure financing from a bank or other lender in order to get started.

4. **Inventory**: You’ll need inventory in order to start selling on Amazon. This could include physical products or digital products such as ebooks or software programs that you create yourself or purchase from another seller on Amazon’s Marketplace platform. 

5. **Fulfillment Services**: If you’re selling physical products on Amazon, you’ll need a fulfillment service in order to store and ship your items quickly and efficiently. There are several third-party fulfillment services available that can help with this process, so make sure you research them thoroughly before making a decision about which one is right for your business needs. 

6. **Customer Service**: Providing excellent customer service is essential for any successful online business, including those selling on Amazon’s Marketplace platform. Make sure you have a system in place for responding quickly and efficiently to customer inquiries and complaints so that customers have a positive experience with your brand every time they interact with it online or offline. 

These are just some of the key prerequisites for starting an Amazon business that you should consider before taking the plunge into ecommerce entrepreneurship!

## Research

Starting an Amazon business can be a great way to make money and build a successful business. However, it is important to do your research before you get started. Knowing the ins and outs of the Amazon marketplace and understanding the different strategies for success can help you maximize your profits and minimize your risks.

First, it is important to understand the different types of Amazon businesses that are available. There are two main types: **Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)** and **Fulfillment by Merchant (FBM)**. **FBA** allows you to store your products in Amazon’s warehouses and have them shipped directly to customers, while **FBM** allows you to list your products on Amazon’s marketplace without having to store them in their warehouses. Knowing which type of business is right for you will help you determine which strategies will work best for your business.

Next, it is important to understand the different fees associated with selling on Amazon. There are fees for listing products, as well as fees for shipping and handling. It is also important to understand how taxes work on Amazon, as this can have a significant impact on your profits. Doing research into these topics will help ensure that you are aware of all costs associated with selling on Amazon before getting started.

Finally, it is important to understand the different strategies that can be used when selling on Amazon. This includes things like pricing strategies, marketing tactics, customer service practices, and more. Doing research into these topics will help ensure that you are taking advantage of all available opportunities when selling on Amazon.

By doing research before starting an Amazon business, you can ensure that you have a better understanding of how the marketplace works and what strategies will be most effective for success. This knowledge can help maximize your profits while minimizing risks associated with starting an online business.

## Selling Plans

Amazon Selling Plans are subscription plans that allow sellers to list and sell products on Amazon.com. The plans offer access to features such as product detail pages, order management, fulfillment services, and more. Sellers can choose from three different plans: Professional, Individual, and Amazon Vendor Central. Each plan has its own set of fees and benefits.
